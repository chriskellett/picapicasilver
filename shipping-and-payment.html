Payment & Shipping

PAYMENT 

Payment in full (including shipping and any special order requests) will be required prior to shipping and/or any special order work commencing. Payment is accepted in the methods provided on this site. We do not accept part or incremental payment and do not offer terms of credit.

Items will not ship until payments have fully processed in our bank.

‍

ORDER CONFIRMATION 

When we receive your order, we send you an email confirming we have received it.

Please allow 1-4 business days for your order to be accepted, processed and dispatched.

All orders are subject to our final acceptance and are only confirmed once we have sent you an email confirming it has been dispatched.  

DELIVERY

When your order has been dispatched please see below for approximate shipping times. We offer free UK only shipping.

Within UK, 2-4 business days.

Your order will be dispatched in a discreet, unbranded external envelope. The jewellery is packaged in a branded Pica Pica Silver jewellery box.

Please note that your package will require a signature. If someone is not present at the time of delivery Royal Mail, will leave a calling card, and you will be able to reschedule delivery for a more convenient time.

We will use our reasonable endeavours to deliver your order within the times indicated. However, where delivery times and dates are given, they are for general guidance only. We will not be held liable for the late delivery of goods caused by circumstances beyond our reasonable control. 

SPECIAL ORDERS

Special Orders include all Pica Pica Silver jewellery that is made-to-order, re-sized and customised in any way. Special Orders are made or customised especially for you; because of this, these pieces of jewellery cannot be returned under any circumstances.

The delivery time for Special Orders may vary and will be confirmed before final purchase. Depending on the item, it may take between 2 and 8 weeks for your jewellery to be handcrafted.

RETURNS

We hope you adore your Pica Pica Silver jewellery. Whilst we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase, if you are not, we will happily offer you a full refund in compliance with our policy.

It is essential to note that, in order to receive a refund, the item(s) must be unworn and in perfect condition (with all (if any) labels attached) and packed within its original packaging. 

Custom-made, custom-sized or re-sized and engraved items: these pieces are Special Orders, which means that the item will be made or customised especially for you; because of this, these pieces of jewellery cannot be returned under any circumstances. This does not affect your statutory rights.

Please see our full Returns and Refunds Policy here.

SERVICING & REPAIRS

We hope you adore your Pica Pica Silver jewellery. Every piece is crafted with fine attention to detail, and to keep it looking as beautiful as the day it arrives, take care when storing and cleaning your jewellery.

REPAIRS

If your jewellery is damaged at any point, we will be very happy to mend any repairable items.

There will be a charge for servicing and repairs, but we do try and keep repair costs to a minimum. Charges for repair will be calculated once we have inspected your piece. You will then be asked to confirm that you'd like to go ahead with the repair. 

SERVICING

If your jewellery is beginning to look a little tired, we will be very happy to bring it back to its former glory. There will be a small charge for services. Any charges will be communicated to you prior to being incurred. 

ARRANGING A SERVICE OR REPAIR

To arrange a repair or to have your jewellery serviced, please contact me.

ONLINE ORDER CANCELLATION

If you need to cancel an order placed online at picapicasilver.co.uk that has not yet been dispatched, please email us straight away. Please note that once your order has been dispatched, we are unable to cancel it. If this is the case, please refer to our returns policy.
