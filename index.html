A bit about me.

Every day I am surrounded by beauty. I live and work in a small historic town in Pembrokeshire, with the National Parks and stunning coastline all around me. And I work with silver and precious stones, creating one of a kind pieces of jewellery.

I draw inspiration for my work everywhere that I look, and I find working in silver is both exciting and meditative at the same time. The pieces I craft are designed to be beautiful every day jewellery. They are elegant and stylish, but with quirky details that make them (and you) stand out.

Each piece of jewellery I make is special to me, and I consider it a real blessing to be able to share my work with you. In a world of mass production, individuality is something to be treasured, and I hope that you find something here to treasure, from my workshop on the tip of Wales.

Rachael
