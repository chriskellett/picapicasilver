Buying from Pica Pica Silver.

Materials.

I only use 925 Solid Sterling Silver for my pieces (no silver plating here).

Hallmarking.

I take great pride in my work so all of my work is Hallmarked by The Goldsmiths Company of London (even the smallest of earrings on their posts).

Packaging and Provenance.

Each item comes in my beautiful Pica Pica Jewellery box with a hand written provenance card written by me.

Delivery.

All items are shipped with tracking and a minimum of £500 insurance (I don't want any of my unique creations getting lost).
